About SIM

Built for Campus Safety

Understanding the purpose and value behind the Security Incident Mapping System.

What is SIM?

SIM is a digital platform that allows the university community to report, track, and manage security incidents across campus in real time.

Objectives

  • Simplify incident reporting
  • Improve response times
  • Centralize security data

Benefits

  • Faster resolution of cases
  • Transparent case tracking
  • Safer campus environment

Target Users

  • Students & Staff
  • Campus Security Office
  • University Administration
